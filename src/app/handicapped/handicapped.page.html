ADA Accessibility Information Parking

There are a limited number of ADA spaces in front of the Koger Center at the drop off area. During ticketed events the metered parking spaces on Assembly Street in front of the Koger Center and on College Street are bagged and reserved as accessible parking as well.

Seating

There are a limited number of accessible seats at the rear of the Orchestra level on both the left and right sides. Please see an Usher for assistance.

Elevators

If you have tickets for seats on the Grand Tier or Balcony levels, there are elevators available at the North end of the Lobby, just past the Assembly Street Entrance from the parking lot. Please see an Usher if you need assistance in reaching or operating the elevator.
